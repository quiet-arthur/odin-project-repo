# Growing and Shrinking 

## What are the 3 values defined in the shorthand flex property (e.g. flex: 1 1 auto)?
    flex-grow: <value>
    flex-shrink: <value>
    flex-basis: <width-value>
## What are the 3 defined values for the flex shorthand flex:auto?
    flex: 1 1 auto
        or 
    flex-grow: 1
    flex-shrink: 1
    flex-basis: auto
